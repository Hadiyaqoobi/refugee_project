Hope Finder

Find scholarships, jobs, and education opportunities — without missing deadlines

Why I Built This

My name is Hadi, and I spent most of my life living as a refugee. While in Turkey, I used to check Google multiple times a day, desperately looking for new opportunities — from online courses to scholarships to jobs.

Eventually, I grew tired of searching and stopped for a while. One day, I discovered I had missed a scholarship deadline that could have changed my life. I don’t want that to happen to anyone else.

So I built this website — to search for refugee-related opportunities for you, and send you a personalized email when new ones appear. You don’t have to miss your chance.

How It Works

  • Register with your email and interests (scholarships, jobs, online courses, etc.)
  • The tool checks trusted websites and search engines daily for new opportunities
  • You get an email as soon as something relevant is found
